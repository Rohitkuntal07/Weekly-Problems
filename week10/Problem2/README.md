# Bitwise AND of Numbers in a Range  

## Problem Statement  
Given two integers **M** and **N**, return the bitwise AND of all numbers in the range `[M, N]` (inclusive).  

Formally:  
result = M & (M+1) & (M+2) & ... & N  

---

## Examples  

### Example 1  
**Input:** M = 5, N = 7 <br>  
**Output:** 4 <br>  
**Explanation:** <br>  
5 = 101 <br>  
6 = 110 <br>  
7 = 111 <br>  
5 & 6 & 7 = 100 = 4 <br>  

---

### Example 2  
**Input:** M = 0, N = 1 <br>  
**Output:** 0 <br>  
**Explanation:** <br>  
0 = 0 <br>  
1 = 1 <br>  
0 & 1 = 0 <br>
